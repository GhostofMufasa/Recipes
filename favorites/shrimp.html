Cóctel de Camaron (Mexican Shrimp Cocktail)

Description

Cóctel de camaron is the type of recipe that hits every taste bud – salty, sweet, tangy, and spicy all wrapped up in each delicious bite! I like to pair it with saltine crackers, but before you know it, I look down and find myself finishing the entire sleeve; it’s that good.

Ingredients

  • Large raw shrimp
  • Clamato
  • Ketchup
  • Lime juice
  • Orange Soda
  • Chopped
  • Chopped vegetables: A mix of finely chopped cucumber, Roma tomatoes, white onion, jalapeño, and chopped cilantro
  • Avacado
  • Kosher Salt
  • Saltines or tostadas

Steps

  1. Poach the shrimp in a large pot with water and kosher salt for 2-3 minutes until the shrimp is plump and pink.
  2. Strain the shrimp and immediately transfer it to an ice bath to cool and prevent it from overcooking. Set aside.
  3. Mix the clamato, ketchup, lime juice, and orange soda until combined. Add in Roma tomatoes, English cucumber, jalapeño, white onion, and chopped cilantro, stirring until all the ingredients are well mixed.
  4. Strain the ice bath and transfer the cooled shrimp into the prepared bowl. Give it a good stir to mix all the ingredients together.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours to let the flavors come together and fully chill the cóctel de camaron.
  6. Add diced avocados and serve with saltines or tostadas.
